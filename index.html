Web Operations Conferences

@Scale

@Scale is a series of technical conferences for engineers who build or maintain systems that are designed for scale. Building applications and services that scale to millions or even billions of people presents a complex set of engineering challenges, many of them unprecedented. The @Scale community is focused on bringing people together to openly discuss these challenges and collaborate on the development of new solutions.

Config Management Camp

ConfigManagementCamp is the event for people interested in open source configuration management to meet each other. ConfigManagementCamp will be the event where different communities will meet and exchange ideas both internally and within the broader community.

Devops Days

The conference that brings development and operations together.

Flowcon

FlowCon brings together technologists and industry leaders passionate about innovation through continuous delivery, continuous design, and lean product development. We'll be exploring the role of culture, technology and design in growing organisations that thrive in an environment of continual change. We will provide inspiring and actionable information for key decision makers responsible for products and services that depend on software.

Gluecon

Gluecon covers topics like NoSQL, APIs, Node.js, HTML5, Backend-as-a-Service, cloud management and security, cloud storage, Hadoop, DevOps, Mobile App development, cloud platforms, and many, many others.

OSCON

Surge

The Surge Conference is the place where Web Infrastructure and Scalability experts gather, advance and emerge. It is where you can access the people and ideas that matter most to your company and career.
